Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes, I enjoy working with startups that have challenging technical problems. I offer flexible engagement models and can work within startup budgets while maintaining high standards.

Can you help with existing blockchain projects?

Absolutely. I specialize in optimizing existing systems, conducting security audits, and helping teams scale their blockchain infrastructure. Many of my clients come with working prototypes that need professional optimization.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer various support models including monthly retainers for ongoing advisory, maintenance contracts, and emergency support for critical systems.

What's your approach to NDA and confidentiality?

I take confidentiality seriously and am happy to sign NDAs before detailed project discussions. All client work is kept strictly confidential unless explicitly agreed otherwise.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

I primarily work remotely but can accommodate on-site work for projects in the Netherlands or short-term engagements in Europe. Remote collaboration has proven highly effective for technical work.

What's included in the free consultation?

The initial consultation includes project assessment, technical feasibility analysis, architecture recommendations, and a clear roadmap with timeline and budget estimates.
